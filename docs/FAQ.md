# RamIA FAQ

## Wallet

**Q: Do I need crypto experience to use RamIA?**  
No. RamIA is designed for first-time users with clear, guided steps.

**Q: Who controls my wallet keys?**  
You do. RamIA is local-first, so key actions happen on your device.

**Q: What if I lose my backup phrase?**  
If the backup is lost, wallet recovery may not be possible. Store your backup safely and privately.

## Coins and mining

**Q: How do I get coins in v1?**  
You can receive starter/test coins through the guided flow to begin using core features.

**Q: Is this guaranteed income or guaranteed mining rewards?**  
No. RamIA does not guarantee earnings, rewards, or profit.

**Q: Can I send coins to other users?**  
Yes. You can send coins, and RamIA shows safety guidance before risky sends.

## Renting bots

**Q: What is bot rental in RamIA?**  
It is an optional feature where users can pay to rent AI bot capability for supported tasks.

**Q: Do I have to rent a bot to use RamIA?**  
No. Bot rental is optional and separate from core wallet use.

**Q: How do payments work for rentals?**  
v1 supports an optional Stripe flow to handle rental payments.

## Privacy

**Q: Is RamIA cloud-only?**  
No. RamIA is local-first for wallet-sensitive operations.

**Q: What data is public?**  
Transaction-level activity may be visible in system records, while local wallet secrets stay on your device if you protect them properly.

**Q: Does RamIA sell my private key data?**  
No. RamIA is designed so private keys stay under your control.

## AI Guardian behavior

**Q: What does AI Guardian do?**  
AI Guardian adds a warning step before potentially risky sends.

**Q: Can AI Guardian block every bad action?**  
No. It provides guidance and warnings, but user confirmation still matters.

**Q: Can I ignore a warning?**  
Depending on flow rules, you may be able to continue after a warning. The warning exists to help you pause and verify details.

## Failure handling

**Q: What if a transaction fails?**  
RamIA should show a clear failure message and let you retry when possible.

**Q: What if Stripe rental checkout fails?**  
You can retry checkout later; no rental is activated until payment succeeds.

**Q: What should I do first if something seems wrong?**  
Pause, verify recipient/payment details, check local app status, and avoid repeated blind retries.
